About Us

Our Mission

Share A Plate was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to connect surplus food with those who need it most, while fostering a community of volunteers dedicated to making a difference.

We believe that no good food should go to waste while people go hungry. By leveraging technology and bringing people together, we're creating a sustainable solution to food waste and hunger.

Our vision is to create a world where food is shared, not wasted, and where communities come together to support one another. Every plate shared is a step towards a more sustainable and compassionate future.
